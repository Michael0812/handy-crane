Handy Crane - Rental with an operator of work transfers and cost reduction

We offer a mini crane rental service with an operator. This is an extremely convenient solution because you do not need to train your own employees, which is known to generate significant costs. Construction equipment is extremely expensive and rent solves this problem. The mini crane is ideally suited as a feeder for heavy building materials, as well as for precision assembly works, such as inserting glass panes. High lifting capacity, mobility, controllability and a long boom ensure safety and accuracy of work.

Mini crane - an experienced operator is the key

Construction projects must be completed within a certain period of time and penalties are imposed for each delay, which is why a construction company should rent heavy equipment together with the operator. This is a guarantee that in the event of a breakdown an experienced employee is available who can deal with it on site, and in the event of a major problem substitute equipment is provided, so there are no delays.
